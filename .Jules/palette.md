## 2025-05-15 - [LanguageToggle and Haptic Feedback]
**Learning:** In a multi-lingual application like Ponloe Creative, providing a clear and accessible way to switch languages is crucial. Integrating haptic feedback (using the custom `hapticLanguageChange` pattern) on mobile provides a delightful tactile confirmation of the language switch, enhancing the overall user experience.
**Action:** Always consider adding haptic feedback to significant state changes (like language or theme toggles) on mobile to provide extra layers of interaction feedback.
